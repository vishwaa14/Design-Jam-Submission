Our Story

Our journey from the bean to the cup started in year 1900. Café culture had not set its footprint and coffee was still considered a luxury. Our quest to provide a truly international experience and brew a truly Guatemala cuppa, led us to create the brand Screamin' Beans.

Spearheading the Indian café culture, Screamin' Beans was an opportunity to discover both the coffee and the craft. No one has mastered coffee better than the Guatemalans. Tipping our hats their way, Screamin' Beans brings new meaning to Guatemala coffee and eats in the Indian sub-continent.

Today, we continue to brew coffee the way world does and in our journey we have created coffee relationships with our customers across India.

SCREAMIN' BEANS PREMIUM ROASTED COFFEE: FROM THE FARM TO YOUR KITCHEN

Screamin' Beans has been growing coffee since 1900´s and is family-owned and operated. We strive to provide the very best coffee Guatemala can offer with multiple varieties of roasted coffee available. When you order your coffee from Screamin' Beans, you’re ordering a fresh batch of Guatemala’s best coffee.

CATUAI AMARELO - 14.11OZ

This variety comes from the crossing Caturra Amarelo and Mundo Novo in Brazil. It is a washed or wet process. This coffee will give you a very nice, clean, elegant cup, well balanced with notes of watermelon and chocolate, nice grapefruit acidity and long aftertaste.



₹1200

ESPRESSO BLEND - 14.11OZ

This blend of our Bourbon and Red Catuai has a considerable body with great regional fruit flavor, clean and complex cup, outstanding acidity and citrus aromas. In espresso, it is ideal to obtain citrus and sweet balances. It is a medium roast for Espresso so that flavors are still in its full! Ideal for espresso machines and milk-based beverages.

₹1200

ROYAL BOURBON - 14.11OZ

One of the Original Arabica varieties, coming from the island of Bourbon now Madagascar. The combination of genetic, microclimate and process results on a delicate body and creamy texture; sweet touches such as jasmine and tropical fruits, balanced cup with good structure and optimal profile for demanding palates.

₹1350

PANDORA PREMIUM PACAMARA - 14.11OZ

Our World Famous Pacamara!It is an excellent creamy body, well balanced, with clean sweetness and tropical fruit flavors, intense peach notes with complex aroma and a post floral and citrus taste giving the palate an experience of unique flavors.
